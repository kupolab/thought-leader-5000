# Thought Leader 5000

**For when you have nothing to say but need to say it.**

Three tools for the LinkedIn-weary:

1. **Build a Post** — Spin the slot machine to assemble the perfect LinkedIn post from hooks, topics, industries, and closers. Generate it with AI, then roast it for good measure.

2. **Roast My Profile** — Paste your real LinkedIn headline and About section. Get a surgical, affectionate roast that names every cliche, detects your LinkedIn archetype, and tells you what your profile is *actually* saying.

3. **Fix My Headline** — Tell us what you actually do and get a real headline with zero buzzwords, zero pipe characters, and zero "passionate about."

## How to use

Open `index.html` in a browser. Enter your Anthropic API key (stored in localStorage, never sent anywhere except Anthropic's API). Pick a mode and go.

No build step. No dependencies. Just one HTML file.
